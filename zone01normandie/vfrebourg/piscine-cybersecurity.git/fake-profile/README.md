### profile-check

#### Goal

Learn to recognize key indicators of fake social media profiles (stock photos, low interaction, inconsistent information, etc.).

#### Instructions

You will be provided with anonymized screenshots or descriptions of multiple online profiles. Based on known indicators, classify whether they are likely genuine or fake, and justify your answer.

#### Deliverables

- A short report (Markdown or PDF) analyzing at least **3 profiles**, identifying indicators and your conclusions.
- Optional: screenshots with annotations if possible.

### bot-signs

#### Goal

Understand how to detect automated (bot) accounts on social media using OSINT patterns.

#### Instructions

Study the behavior and publishing patterns of suspected bot accounts. Focus on signs such as posting frequency, content repetition, language patterns, and follower ratios.

#### Deliverables

- A short summary of behavioral indicators common to bots.
- An analysis of **1 or 2 suspected bot accounts** (screenshots, links, or detailed descriptions).

### deep-dive

#### Goal

Perform an in-depth OSINT investigation on a single profile to map out activity, interactions, and metadata.

#### Instructions

Select a publicly available profile (or use a provided anonymized one). Investigate timeline, interactions, geolocation clues, photo metadata, etc.

#### Deliverables

- A report summarizing key findings.
- Screenshots and tools used (or methodology if screenshots can't be shared).

### timeline-build

#### Goal

Build a coherent and accurate timeline of a person's online activity based on available OSINT data.

#### Instructions

Using data from one or more sources (e.g., Twitter, Facebook, blogs, YouTube), construct a timeline of events or posts made by a target.

#### Deliverables

- A clear chronological timeline (in table, markdown, or spreadsheet form).
- A short paragraph explaining the information sources and gaps if any.
