### banner-audit

#### Goal

Evaluate a website’s cookie banner for GDPR compliance.

#### Instructions

Pick a real website (non-login page) and review the cookie banner. Check for:

- Visibility and clarity of the banner
- Clear options to accept/refuse cookies
- Link to cookie policy or settings
- Whether tracking cookies are set before consent

Take screenshots to support your findings.

#### Deliverables

- A short report with screenshots and commentary.

### consent-check

#### Goal

Test whether a site respects cookie consent settings.

#### Instructions

Use browser dev tools or extensions (like Cookie Editor, Consent-O-Matic) to inspect:

- What cookies are set before and after consent
- Whether "reject all" really disables trackers
- If cookie settings persist on reload or revisit

Choose a single site, test in incognito, and document everything.

#### Deliverables

- A technical note with cookie names, behaviors, and tools used.

### cookie-scan

#### Goal

Perform a detailed cookie inspection of a website.

#### Instructions

Use browser tools or a scanner (e.g., Webbkoll, CookieServe) to extract:

- All cookies set on page load
- Domains and paths
- Expiration dates
- Third-party trackers or ad services

Choose any public site. Document your process.

#### Deliverables

- A list of cookies, their properties, and relevant analysis.
