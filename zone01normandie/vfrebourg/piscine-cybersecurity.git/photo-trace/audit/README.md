## exif-pull

## exif-pull – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student extracted EXIF metadata from at least 2 images?

###### Are timestamps, camera/device model, or GPS coordinates included?

###### Does the student explain what the metadata reveals?

### Bonus

###### + Is any geolocation attempted based on GPS?

###### + Does the student use a CLI tool (like exiftool) with screenshots?

## geo-clues

## geo-clues – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student attempted to analyze geo-indicators in 2 images?

###### Are clues like language, buildings, traffic signs, or environment used?

###### Is there a clear reasoning for proposed locations?

### Bonus

###### + Are reverse searches or map tools used to confirm hypotheses?

###### + Are screenshots with annotations included?

---

## img-rev

## img-rev – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student performed reverse search on 2 images?

###### Are the results summarized and links/screenshots included?

###### Has the student identified original sources or similar postings?

### Bonus

###### + Are there attempts to trace aliases or accounts based on images?

###### + Are tool comparisons (e.g., Yandex vs Google) discussed?

---

## source-hunt

## source-hunt – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student attempted to trace image origin?

###### Are multiple tools used (e.g., TinEye + social media + metadata)?

###### Is a chronological timeline or evidence trail presented?

### Bonus

###### + Is the exact original post or poster identified?

###### + Does the student use archive services (e.g., Wayback Machine)?
