### exif-pull

#### Goal

Extract metadata (EXIF) from digital images to reveal hidden information like GPS, timestamp, or device type.

#### Instructions

Use tools such as `exiftool`, `mat2`, or online EXIF viewers to analyze at least **two images** and extract their metadata.

#### Deliverables

- A short report describing the metadata retrieved.
- Interpret any useful information (e.g., device, date, GPS).

### img-rev

#### Goal

Use reverse image search to find similar images or original sources.

#### Instructions

Use services like `Google Images`, `TinEye`, or `Yandex` to perform a reverse search on **2 different images**.

#### Deliverables

- A summary of the results for each image.
- URLs or screenshots from the reverse search tools.

### geo-clues

#### Goal

Identify location hints in a photo without metadata.

#### Instructions

Analyze 2 images and try to determine possible geographic clues based on visual content (architecture, signs, vegetation, clothing, vehicles, etc.).

#### Deliverables

- A report describing visible indicators and your hypotheses.
- Optional: annotated screenshots or external references (e.g., Google Street View).

### source-hunt

#### Goal

Trace the original source of an image shared online.

#### Instructions

Choose an image and attempt to find where it was first posted online. Combine tools: reverse image search, social media search, EXIF, and timestamp comparison.

#### Deliverables

- A timeline of where the image appeared.
- Summary of methods used to identify the likely source.
