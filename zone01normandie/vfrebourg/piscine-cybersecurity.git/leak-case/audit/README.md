## breach-story

## breach-story – Audit

### Functional

##### Does the student identify the correct data type and attack vector?

###### Are timeline and response failures clearly listed?

###### Are GDPR/compliance issues correctly referenced?

### Bonus

###### + Are mitigation or preventive measures discussed?

###### + Is the answer well-structured with headings or bullets?

## fix-plan

## fix-plan – Audit

### Functional

##### Are at least 3 short-term and 3 long-term actions provided?

###### Are both technical and procedural fixes listed?

###### Are training or governance topics covered?

### Bonus

###### + Is the format professional and presentation-ready?

###### + Is budget or priority estimation included?

## notify-dpa – Audit

### Functional

## user-info

## user-info – Audit

### Functional

##### Does the email include all key elements?

###### Is the tone user-friendly and not alarmist?

###### Are instructions to users clear and actionable?

### Bonus

###### + Is formatting close to a real HTML/text email?

###### + Are phishing risks or scams mentioned in advice?
