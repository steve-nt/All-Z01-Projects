### breach-story

#### Goal

Analyze a fictional data breach scenario and identify key timeline elements and security flaws.

#### Instructions

You are given a short narrative describing a data breach involving a CRM system. From the story, identify:

- The type of data exposed
- The initial attack vector
- Detection timeline
- Failure points in incident response
- Compliance risks (e.g., GDPR articles breached)

#### Deliverables

- A document with your analysis (max 2 pages, bullet points allowed).

### notify-dpa

#### Goal

Draft a simulated breach report for a data protection authority (DPA).

#### Instructions

Using the breach scenario from `breach-story`, write a short DPA notification (max 1 page) that includes:

- Nature of the breach
- Affected data types and volume
- Potential consequences
- Measures taken or planned
- Contact point at your organization

Use a formal tone.

#### Deliverables

- A simulated DPA report in PDF or Markdown.

### user-info

#### Goal

Write an email to notify affected users of the breach.

#### Instructions

You must write an email informing users that their data was potentially exposed. The email must include:

- A summary of what happened
- What data was affected
- What actions are being taken
- How users can protect themselves
- A contact point for questions

The tone should be transparent and reassuring.

#### Deliverables

- A sample notification email (1 page max).

### fix-plan

#### Goal

Propose remediations after the breach.

#### Instructions

Using the same scenario from `breach-story`, list concrete remediations that your company should take. Include:

- Short-term and long-term actions
- Technical and organizational fixes
- Training or policy updates
- Preventive monitoring or detection improvements

#### Deliverables

- A remediation plan (structured document or table).
