## fill-steps

#### Functional

##### Does the student correctly reuse and adapt the template from the previous project?

##### Is the phishing scenario well described and realistic?

##### Are the containment, eradication, and recovery steps detailed and specific to phishing?

##### Is the communication plan adapted to the scenario?

##### Is the playbook written in clear, structured English and easy to follow?

#### Bonus

###### + Does the playbook anticipate potential complications (e.g., lateral movement, multiple users impacted)?

###### + Is there a reference to email forensic analysis or phishing indicators?

###### + Are there team roles clearly identified for each action step?

###### + Does the student include metrics or criteria for successful recovery?

## share-doc

#### Functional

##### Does the document reuse and adapt the contents of `phishing_playbook.md`?

##### Is the structure clear and easy to read (headers, bullet points, spacing)?

##### Is a short introduction included to explain the purpose of the document?

##### Is there a changelog or metadata section at the end of the file?

##### Is the file named correctly and suitable for internal distribution?

#### Bonus

###### + Does the document include internal links or a table of contents?

###### + Has the student added visual elements (e.g., ASCII dividers, warning icons, etc.) to improve clarity?

###### + Is the document written in a professional and neutral tone?

###### + Is there mention of how the document will be maintained or reviewed over time?
