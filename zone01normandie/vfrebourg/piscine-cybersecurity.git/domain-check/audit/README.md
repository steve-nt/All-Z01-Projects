## history-dig

## history-dig – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student built a history for one domain?

###### Are key dates or changes in ownership/content identified?

###### Has the student used at least one online tool like Wayback Machine?

###### Are the results interpreted (e.g., linked to suspicious behavior)?

### Bonus

###### + Does the student reference previous WHOIS info or screenshots?

###### + Is a visual timeline or table used to present findings?

## reputation-check

## reputation-check – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student checked reputation for at least one IP and one domain?

###### Are at least 2 online services referenced (e.g., VirusTotal, AbuseIPDB)?

###### Does the student explain if the IP/domain is considered safe or malicious?

###### Are screenshots or result links provided?

### Bonus

###### + Is there a cross-check between tools for accuracy?

###### + Does the student suggest mitigation steps or indicators of compromise (IOC)?

---

## url-hunt

## url-hunt – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student investigated at least 2 suspicious or shortened URLs?

###### Are final destinations correctly resolved and analyzed?

###### Were redirections or content changes noted?

###### Were tools like unshorten.me, urlscan.io or sandboxing used?

### Bonus

###### + Does the student mention if any URLs lead to known bad actors or phishing?

###### + Are redirection chains illustrated or documented clearly?

## whois-scan

## whois-scan – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student analyzed at least 2 domains using WHOIS?

###### Are registrar, creation/expiry dates, and DNS info identified?

###### Does the student interpret the results (e.g., anonymity, duration)?

###### Are the tools used clearly mentioned?

### Bonus

###### + Is there a comparison between domains?

###### + Does the student provide a screenshot or CLI result from `whois`?
