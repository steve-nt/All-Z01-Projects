### whois-scan

#### Goal

Learn to use WHOIS lookups for identifying domain ownership and registration details.

#### Instructions

Use tools such as `whois`, `whoxy.com`, `domaintools.com`, or other OSINT services to analyze at least **two domains**. Look for registrar info, creation/expiry dates, contacts, and DNS servers.

#### Deliverables

- A short report for each analyzed domain, listing relevant WHOIS fields and your interpretation.

### history-dig

#### Goal

Investigate the past of a domain to detect suspicious history or ownership changes.

#### Instructions

Use tools like `wayback machine`, `securitytrails`, or `whois history` to reconstruct the history of at least **one domain**.

#### Deliverables

- A timeline or summary of major changes (hosting, registrant, content).
- Screenshots or descriptions of past snapshots.

### reputation-check

#### Goal

Assess the reputation of an IP or domain using online blacklists or threat intelligence.

#### Instructions

Use services like `virustotal.com`, `urlscan.io`, `abuseipdb.com`, `talosintelligence.com`, or `phishtank.com` to analyze **one IP and one domain**.

#### Deliverables

- A short report summarizing the reputational data from multiple sources.
- Screenshots or raw results from the lookup tools.

### url-hunt

#### Goal

Investigate shortened, obfuscated, or suspicious URLs to identify risk or redirection.

#### Instructions

Analyze at least **two suspicious URLs** using tools such as `unshorten.me`, `redirect-checker.org`, `urlscan.io`, or browser sandboxing.

#### Deliverables

- A brief report for each URL: final destination, risks, and analysis steps.
- Optional: screenshots of tools or redirection chains.
