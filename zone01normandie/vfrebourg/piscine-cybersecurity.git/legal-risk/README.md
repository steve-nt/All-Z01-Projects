### who-liable

#### Goal

Identify the responsible party in a data incident.

#### Instructions

You’re given a scenario:

> A third-party mailing service used by your company leaked user email addresses. The service failed to encrypt data, and the contract didn’t include specific security clauses.

Determine:

- Who is legally liable?
- What are the obligations of each party?
- What documentation could reduce exposure?

#### Deliverables

- A short legal analysis (max 1 page).

### fine-types

#### Goal

Understand types and ranges of GDPR penalties.

#### Instructions

Study the two levels of GDPR fines and explain:

- What triggers each level?
- Examples of behaviors under each
- Maximum amounts and % of turnover

Make it educational — this will feed into future playbooks.

#### Deliverables

- A summarized explanation or comparison table.

### case-study

#### Goal

Analyze a real GDPR fine decision.

#### Instructions

Choose one GDPR enforcement case from:

- [https://www.enforcementtracker.com/](https://www.enforcementtracker.com/)

Summarize:

- Who was fined and why
- Which GDPR articles were breached
- The final penalty and justification
- Your takeaway

#### Deliverables

- A structured summary of the case (500 words max).
