## cross-ref

## cross-ref – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student selected 3–5 data points to cross-check?

###### Are two or more sources cited per data point?

###### Is the validation process explained clearly?

### Bonus

###### + Are conflicting sources discussed and compared?

###### + Are tools or automation scripts used for validation?

## data-timeline

## data-timeline – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student organized at least 5 key findings into a timeline?

###### Are dates and source references included?

###### Is the format clear and accessible (markdown, table, spreadsheet)?

### Bonus

###### + Is a visual timeline or diagram included?

###### + Are discrepancies between sources noted?

## find-target

## find-target – Audit

### Functional

##### Has the student selected or generated a fictional identity?

###### Has basic OSINT been performed (social media, usernames, emails)?

###### Are methods and findings documented clearly?

### Bonus

###### + Are multiple tools and platforms compared?

###### + Does the student simulate ethical reporting?

---

## report-out

## report-out – Audit

### Functional

##### Is the report well-structured and readable?

###### Are findings summarized clearly with supporting evidence?

###### Is the tone professional and objective?

### Bonus

###### + Is a table of contents or annex included?

###### + Is there a discussion of limitations or further research?
