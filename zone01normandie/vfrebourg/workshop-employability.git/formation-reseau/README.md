#### Extracurricular Project: Networking Workshop

##### Description

The Networking Workshop teaches learners how to expand and strengthen their professional network, primarily using LinkedIn. Participants will learn strategies for connecting with professionals, engaging with relevant content, and positioning themselves as active members in their industry. The objective is to help them build meaningful professional relationships that can support their career development.

---

### What is expected?

##### General Information

###### Has the participant updated their LinkedIn profile to improve their visibility?
###### Have they engaged with professional content by commenting or sharing relevant posts?

---  

##### Networking Growth

###### Has the participant increased their number of LinkedIn connections with relevant professionals?
###### Can they demonstrate how they initiated conversations or built new professional relationships?
###### Have they joined professional groups or communities related to their field?
