### Extracurricular Project: Create Your Portfolio

#### Description
The **Create Your Portfolio** project ensures that learners have a complete and well-structured professional portfolio, including a resume and a portfolio website. This validation process checks that the portfolio effectively showcases the learner’s skills, experiences, and projects in a professional manner. The goal is to help learners present themselves convincingly to potential employers by ensuring they have the necessary materials to stand out in the job market.

---

### What is expected?

##### Contact Information Presence

- Does the CV include name, first name, email, and phone number?
- Does the CV include professional links (GitHub, LinkedIn, Portfolio, Personal website)?

##### Professional Experiences and Projects

- Does the CV mention detailed professional experiences (internships, apprenticeships, full-time jobs, freelance)?
- Are curriculum projects well-detailed (context, technologies used, role, results)?

##### Presentation of Skills

- Does the CV list relevant technical skills (languages, frameworks, tools, databases)?
- Are behavioral skills (communication, project management, autonomy, teamwork) highlighted?

##### Adaptation to Companies

- Has the learner created 3 CVs tailored to 3 different companies (sector, mission, values)?

##### Clarity of Layout

- Is the layout clear and spacious?
- Are sections well-organized (experiences, skills, education, etc.)?
- Are ATS-friendly keywords used?
- Does the CV adhere to an optimal length (1 to 2 pages maximum)?
- Are spelling and grammar impeccable?

##### Portfolio Verification

- Has the learner created a portfolio?
- Is the portfolio accessible online? Is the link available on the repository of this project?
- Does the portfolio include essential information (CV, projects, contact details, relevant links)?
- Is the portfolio visually appealing and easy to navigate?

##### Hook and Differentiation

- Does the CV include a compelling hook (title, objective, introduction sentence)?

#### Bonus

- Does the CV feature a professional photo suitable for the industry?
- Does the CV include relevant personal projects?
- Are additional certifications or courses mentioned?
