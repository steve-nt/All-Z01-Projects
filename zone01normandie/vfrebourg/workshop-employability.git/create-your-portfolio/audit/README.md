### Extracurricular Project: Create Your Portfolio
#### Description

The *Create Your Portfolio* project guides the participant through designing and publishing a personal portfolio website. The goal is to ensure that learners can present their skills, projects, and professional identity through a clear, accessible, and functional online portfolio.

---
### What is expected?

##### General Information

###### Can the participant explain the purpose of a personal portfolio and its importance?
###### Can they describe the core sections typically included in a portfolio (About, Skills, Projects, Contact, etc.)?

---

##### Portfolio Creation

###### Has the participant successfully built a functional portfolio website?
###### Is the portfolio accessible online through a public URL?
###### Does the portfolio clearly present at least one project with a description and visuals?
###### Does the participant understand how to update or maintain their portfolio in the future?
