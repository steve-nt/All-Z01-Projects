### Extracurricular Project: Technical Pitch Workshop

#### Description

The Technical Pitch Workshop helps learners effectively present their technical skills in a structured and compelling way. Participants will learn how to articulate their expertise, showcase their past projects, and demonstrate their problem-solving abilities with concrete examples. The objective is to develop their ability to confidently communicate their technical knowledge, an essential skill for interviews and professional growth.

---

### What is expected?

##### General Information

###### Can the participant clearly explain their main technical skills?
###### Can they provide real-world examples of how they have applied these skills?

---

##### Pitching Skills

###### Can the participant effectively present a past project, explaining their role and contributions?
###### Are they able to break down complex technical concepts for different audiences?
###### Can they confidently answer technical questions related to their expertise?

---

##### Demonstration & Engagement

###### Can the participant demonstrate a skill or project live (e.g., coding, debugging, setup)?
###### Are they able to adapt their presentation based on feedback or follow-up questions?
###### Do they showcase confidence and clarity when discussing technical topics?
