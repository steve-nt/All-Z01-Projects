### Extracurricular Project: Pitching Workshop

#### Description

The Pitching Workshop is designed to help learners effectively present and sell their training experience in professional discussions and interviews. Participants will practice articulating the value of their education at Zone01, explaining key skills acquired, and making a compelling case for their expertise. The objective is to enhance their ability to confidently market themselves in a professional setting.

---

### What is expected?

##### General Information

###### Can the participant clearly explain what Zone01 is and what makes it unique?
###### Can they effectively highlight the key skills acquired during the training?

---

##### Pitching Skills

###### Can the participant confidently deliver a structured pitch about their formation at Zone01?
###### Are they able to adapt their pitch depending on the audience (technical vs non-technical)?
###### Do they demonstrate enthusiasm and confidence while presenting?
