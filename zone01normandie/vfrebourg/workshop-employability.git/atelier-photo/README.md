### Extracurricular Project: Professional Photo Workshop

#### Description

The Professional Photo Workshop provides learners with the opportunity to take high-quality, professional photos for use on their LinkedIn profiles and other professional platforms. The goal is to help them establish a strong and polished online presence, which is essential for networking and career opportunities.

---

### What is expected?

##### General Information

###### Has the participant taken a professional-quality photo during the workshop?
###### Have they updated their LinkedIn profile and resume with their new professional photo?
