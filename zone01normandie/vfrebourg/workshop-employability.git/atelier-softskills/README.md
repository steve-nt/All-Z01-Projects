### Extracurricular Project: Soft Skills Workshop

#### Description

The Soft Skills Workshop focuses on helping learners effectively present their interpersonal and professional skills both in writing and verbally. Participants will learn how to articulate their strengths in communication, teamwork, adaptability, and problem-solving, making them more confident and prepared for professional interactions.

---

### What is expected?

##### General Information

###### Can the participant list and explain their key soft skills?
###### Can they provide concrete examples of situations where they have used these skills?

---

##### Presentation Skills

###### Can the participant effectively describe their soft skills in a written format (e.g., resume, LinkedIn profile)?
###### Are they able to present their soft skills confidently in an oral presentation?
###### Do they demonstrate adaptability by tailoring their soft skills pitch to different audiences?
