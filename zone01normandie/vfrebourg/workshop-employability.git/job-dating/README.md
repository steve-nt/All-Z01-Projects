### Extracurricular Project: Job Dating Workshop

#### Description

The Job Dating Workshop provides participants with the opportunity to practice job interviews through a series of short, structured meetings with HR students or industry professionals. The objective is to help learners improve their interview skills, gain confidence, and receive constructive feedback in a real-world scenario.  

---

### What is expected?

##### General Information

###### Has the participant attended the workshop and participated in multiple interviews?
###### Did they complete approximately ten interviews with HR students or professionals?
###### Have they received feedback on their interview performance?
